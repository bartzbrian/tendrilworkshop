Hello, my name is Brian and I've been working in the Bay Area as a gardener for years, helping out with all sorts of different garden projects, all the way from simple planting to full scale landscaping transformations.

Over the years I have also worked at notable Bay Area plant nurseries including Annie's Annuals and Perennials, Broadway Terrace Nursery, and Yarrow Nursery, where I gained vast knowledge about local plant life and horticultural practices.

I was trained as an artist, and work as a part time professor in design at the University of San Francisco whenever I'm not gardening. I'm looking forward to helping you with your garden needs!


I provide garden services in and near Berkeley, Oakland, and San Francisco. I offer free consultations, and am available to help with any aspect of your garden. Services offered include:


  • Garden Design
  • Installation and Planting
  • Maintainence and Cleanup
  • Soil Amendment
  • Pest Management
  • Hauling and Removal
  • Defensible Space Compliance
  • Hardscaping
  • Drip Irrigation
