About

Hello! My name is Brian. Tendril Workshop is a natural outgrowth of my art practice. My focus here is to create objects which live practical lives, incorporating my ongoing fascination with the plant-world into everyday objects for you (yes you!) to interact with.

All items are handmade by me, one at a time. I aim to fulfill orders within 2 weeks, unless otherwise noted (some items will have a longer lead time). Please feel free to reach out with questions, or to inquire about purchasing anything which is sold out. All sales are final. Please MAKE SURE you have entered the correct address. I won't be able to replace anything that's sent to a wrong address or stolen. Local pickup available in the Bay Area!!

I am open for custom work and commissions as well! This can be anything from a color/finish variation on an already existing item, to a custom earring, or an object neverbefore seen! Drop me a line and let's chat about it.
